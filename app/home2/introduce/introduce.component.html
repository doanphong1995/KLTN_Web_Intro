Some information


BOM SHOP is a professional floral and gift delivery company with more than 64 franchise retail locations all over Vietnam. Our mission is to provide the most convenient ways to help people express their feeling: love, congratulation, appreciation, adoration, condolences, gratitude, etc. With the slogan "Flower Delivery Expert", our bouquet is not only a gift, but also a message of love and sharing...


Our orientations focus on these activities:

♥  Connecting and supporting the flower shops in the country to provide fresh flowers promptly.

♥  Building a professional floral delivery networt in all regions of Viet Nam.

♥  Improving quality of delivery process Providing successful models in flowers business to all franchise retail locations.

♥  Focusing on providing domestic flowers and materials.

♥  Developing the ancillary services to support flowers and gifts delivery services.

♥  Researching and applying Information Technology in the development of fresh flowers business.


"Love" is taken from one of the most important values of Christianity. It expresses love for not only couples but also for humanity. Therefore, with each flower sold, we will donate a small amount for "Help children get to school" Fund.!
